Elisenda Torras. Barcelona, Fashion & Art.

I’m a passionate, innovative and social entrepreneur born in Barcelona and now living in London, who loves fashion and enjoys connecting people. Sanam Balani. Tenerife, Technology & Music.

Having studied Law, interned in Technology and now working in Investment Banking, I couldn't be more different to your tech-savvy coder. I love reading up on disruptive technology and am keen on exploring ways to disrupt and challenge the social norm, both in and outside of work.
